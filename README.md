# Little Chef Academy by Autasha Smith

A warm, family-friendly brand and web project centered around teaching children cooking skills through fun, hands-on experiences that build confidence, creativity, and practical life skills.

---

## Overview

**Little Chef Academy by Autasha Smith** is a children’s cooking brand designed to create joyful, enriching, and memorable culinary experiences for kids. This repository serves as the central home for the project’s branding, landing page development, waitlist strategy, design direction, and future growth assets.

The goal of this project is to launch a polished digital presence that introduces the brand, builds trust with parents, and captures interest through a waiting list for upcoming classes, workshops, camps, and special cooking events.

---

## Vision

Little Chef Academy exists to help children discover the joy of cooking in a way that is safe, creative, confidence-building, and fun.

The long-term vision is to grow beyond a simple landing page into a trusted family brand that may include:

## Design Principles

The project should follow these design principles:

1. **Trust first**  
   Parents should immediately feel that the brand is safe, thoughtful, and professional.

2. **Warmth and personality**  
   The brand should feel personal and approachable, not cold or overly corporate.

3. **Clear value**  
   The website should quickly communicate why cooking lessons matter for children.

4. **Simple conversion path**  
   Joining the waiting list should feel easy and inviting.

5. **Room to grow**  
   The design and messaging should support long-term expansion.

## Google Sheet Waitlist Integration

This repo now includes a Google Apps Script endpoint template in `google-apps-script.gs` for collecting waitlist submissions from the landing page form.

### Setup steps
1. Create or open your destination Google Sheet.
2. Open **Extensions → Apps Script**, then paste in the contents of `google-apps-script.gs`.
3. Deploy as a **Web app**:
   - Execute as: **Me**
   - Who has access: **Anyone**
4. Copy the deployed Web App URL.
5. In `little-chef-academy.html` (and `index.html`), replace:
   - `https://script.google.com/macros/s/REPLACE_WITH_YOUR_WEB_APP_ID/exec`
   with your deployed Web App URL.
